Spoken offers two distinct paragraph modes to format Scripture for clarity and flow:

üìö USFM-Based Paragraphing

Structured by traditional formatting standards using the Unified Standard Format Marker (USFM) system ‚Äî a globally recognized markup standard used by Bible translators and publishers. It preserves:

  • Official paragraph breaks from source manuscripts
  • Consistent formatting across translations
  • Study-friendly structure for referencing and cross-comparison

Ideal for readers who want to stay close to the original editorial layout while still enjoying a clean, prose-like experience.

ü§ñ AI Coherent Idea Paragraphing

Grouped by meaning, not just formatting. This mode uses AI to analyze the semantic flow of Scripture ‚Äî clustering verses into natural idea units based on context, theme, and narrative rhythm. It helps you:

  • Follow complete thoughts across verse boundaries
  • See dialogue and arguments unfold without interruption
  • Experience poetry and prophecy with literary clarity

Perfect for immersive reading, thematic study, and devotional reflection.

Why It Matters: Whether you're reading Paul's letters, tracing a parable, or exploring the Psalms, semantic paragraphing helps you understand Scripture as a coherent message, engage with meaning, not just structure, and choose the format that fits your purpose.
