Professional

Today, I serve as the Strategic Director and Principal Advisor for several private companies. I previously served as CEO of Fiddlesticks Wood Products, a manufacturer of cooking and fuelwood products, where I engineered and orchestrated the vertical integration of functions into divisions, entities, and partners.

The result was a highly controlled internal supply chain, encompassing:

  • Green Yard Sourcing – Collection and preprocessing of recycled content
  • Manufacturing – Conversion of log truckloads into processed products
  • Packaging – On-premises processing and co-packer management
  • Wholesale Distribution – Packaged fuelwood to Mass Market Retail
  • Alabama Cooking Wood – Brand-specific distribution to restaurants
  • Direct-to-Consumer E-commerce – Tailored products & fulfillment systems

My experience spans diverse industries and roles. A natural curiosity drives me to analyze complex problems and deliver solutions through collaboration. From factory floor to balance sheet, I value strategy, integration, and outcomes. Check out my CV if you want to know more →

Philosophy

Personal development demands honesty about limitations, failures, and vanities. I believe in truth, discipline, and growth. Virtue is demanding—integrity even more so. My aim is to be fruitful, subdue myself, and produce something worthwhile.
